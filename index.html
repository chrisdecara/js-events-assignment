A Not-so-Scary Delve into JavaScript

by Christopher De Cara

Welcome to a "not-so-scary" delve into the world of JavaScript! On this page, I learned how to manipulate images using JavaScript, so that you, the person viewing this site, are able to interact with it in a meaningful way. Seriously, give it a try! On the first image, just give it a click. On the second image, just hover your cursor over it, and then take your cursor off. Oh, and don't let the off-putting appearance of these two turn you away.
